--A materialized view physically saves query results to disk for speed, but when the underlying data changes,it become outdated so therefore it must be updated efficiently using incremental modifications. Because the view filters for distinct part numbers (DISTINCT partNo) where the cost exceeds 1000, it can be maintained without ever accessing the base table during insertions by simply adding new parts or ignoring irrelevant ones. However, during deletions or downward price updates, the view becomes blind and does not have access if other valid contracts still exist for that part; to prevent a costly scan of the base table to check, the system must maintain a hidden derivation counter alongside each part number to safely track duplicates and manage deletions entirely within the view.
